# How to contribute!

* You can directly contribute by writing or reviewing [pull requests.](https://github.com/TYKUHN2/AtomicFramework/pulls)
* You can participate in [polls and discussions](https://github.com/TYKUHN2/AtomicFramework/discussions) about the direction of the project.
* You can simply [use the framework](https://github.com/TYKUHN2/AtomicFramework/releases/latest)
and [report issues and suggestions](https://github.com/TYKUHN2/AtomicFramework/issues) that I may not be aware of.
* Have a security concern? [Report it](https://github.com/TYKUHN2/AtomicFramework/security) using our vulnerability board.
